# Stakeholder Update
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-executive
**Command:** /stakeholder-update

## What it does
Drafts clear, appropriately detailed stakeholder updates for investors, board members, key customers, or internal leadership. Takes a set of bullet points and shapes them into a narrative that leads with the headline, provides necessary context, addresses risks honestly, and closes with a clear ask or next step.

## When to use
- Monthly investor update emails
- End-of-quarter internal leadership communications
- Customer-facing project status updates

## Example output
**Monthly Investor Update — March 2026**

**Headline:** Strong February close; sales pipeline at record levels heading into Q2

**Key updates:**

*Revenue:* February closed at $1.24M MRR (+8% MoM, +61% YoY). Net revenue retention for the trailing 12 months is 118%. Churn ticked up slightly (1.4% gross MRR churn vs. 1.1% in January) — one mid-market customer non-renewed; identified as an implementation failure we're addressing with a new onboarding playbook.

*Pipeline:* Sales pipeline stands at $8.2M in qualified opportunities, the highest in company history. 14 enterprise deals are in late-stage evaluation. We expect 4-6 to close in Q2.

*Product:* The workflow automation module shipped on March 10, two weeks ahead of plan. Early customer feedback is strongly positive; NPS for new activations is running at 67.

*Team:* VP of Customer Success started March 3. Three engineering roles remain open; offers extended to two candidates.

**Where we need your help:**
- Introductions to CFOs at mid-market RIAs in the Southeast — two specific targets in your network (names attached)
- Any portfolio company references for our enterprise security review process

Next update: April 7.
