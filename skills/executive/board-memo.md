# Board Memo
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-executive
**Command:** /board-memo

## What it does
Drafts executive-quality board memos and pre-read documents from a set of inputs: topic, key data points, decision needed (if any), context, and risks. Produces a concise, structured document in the standard board memo format: situation, background, analysis, recommendation, and appendix pointer.

## When to use
- Preparing a board update on a strategic initiative
- Requesting board approval for a significant decision
- Summarizing a complex situation for non-operational board members

## Example output
**BOARD MEMO**
**To:** Board of Directors
**From:** [CEO Name]
**Date:** March 2026
**Re:** Strategic Partnership with Verdant Capital — Approval Requested

**Situation:**
Management is seeking board approval to enter a strategic partnership with Verdant Capital that would provide $15M in structured growth financing and co-marketing rights to Verdant's 800-firm RIA network.

**Background:**
Since Q3 2025, management has evaluated seven potential capital partners. Verdant emerged as the strongest fit based on network alignment, term flexibility, and minimal governance intrusion. Full due diligence was completed in February 2026.

**Key Terms:**
- $15M structured note, 5-year term, 7% coupon
- Verdant receives preferred distribution rights to their RIA network for 36 months
- No board seat; quarterly reporting obligation

**Risks and Mitigations:**
- Channel conflict risk: mitigated by geographic exclusion carveouts (Appendix B)
- Dependency risk: 36-month exclusivity is time-limited and scope-limited

**Recommendation:**
Management recommends approval. This partnership accelerates our distribution strategy by an estimated 18 months while preserving operational independence.

**Requested action:** Approve resolution authorizing CEO to execute the partnership agreement.
