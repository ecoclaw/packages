# Email Newsletter

## What this skill does

Produces a complete monthly email newsletter designed to:
- Keep the firm top-of-mind with existing clients
- Educate prospects and move them toward consultation
- Establish thought leadership
- Drive traffic back to website content

Includes:
1. **Subject line variants** (5 options, A/B test the top 2)
2. **Preview text** (matched to each subject line)
3. **Full newsletter body** — 400-600 words, mobile-optimized structure
4. **CTA section** — primary and secondary call to action
5. **Footer copy** — disclaimer, unsubscribe language, contact info

## How to invoke

```
/email-newsletter
```

Claude will ask:
- What month is this for?
- What are 2-3 things that happened this month worth mentioning? (regulatory updates, firm news, a case win, a published article)
- Is there a primary topic or theme?
- Any client segment this is targeting? (all clients / developers / municipalities / businesses)
- What's the main CTA? (schedule consult, read blog post, download guide)

## Newsletter structure

```
[SUBJECT LINE]
[PREVIEW TEXT]

---
[HEADER: Firm logo + "Monthly Update | [Month Year]"]

[OPENING — 2-3 sentences. What's happening in environmental law this month that readers need to know?]

---
⚖️ REGULATORY UPDATE
[1-2 paragraphs on a relevant regulatory change, deadline, or enforcement trend]
[Link: "Read our full analysis →"]

---
📋 WHAT WE'VE BEEN WORKING ON
[Brief, non-confidential mention of the types of matters the firm handled — no client names]
[1-2 sentences on a firm milestone, speaking engagement, or article]

---
💡 QUICK TIP FOR [TARGET CLIENT TYPE]
[Practical tip in 3-5 bullet points — actionable, specific, valuable even without hiring the firm]

---
📖 FROM THE BLOG
[Blog post title + 2-sentence summary + link]
[Blog post title + 2-sentence summary + link]

---
📞 FREE CONSULTATION
[CTA block: problem statement → solution → button "Schedule a Consultation"]

---
[FOOTER: disclaimer, address, unsubscribe]
```
