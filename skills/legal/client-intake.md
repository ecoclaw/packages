# Client Intake
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-legal
**Command:** /client-intake

## What it does
Generates a structured client intake questionnaire and summary template tailored to a specific practice area (litigation, transactional, employment, estate planning, etc.). Intake questions are sequenced to surface conflicts, gather key facts, and identify urgency without overwhelming new clients.

## When to use
- Onboarding a new client in an unfamiliar matter type
- Standardizing intake across your practice
- Delegating initial intake to staff or a client portal form

## Example output
**Client Intake — Employment Dispute (Employee-Side)**

1. Describe the situation in your own words. What happened, and when did it start?
2. Are you currently employed at this company? If not, what was your last day?
3. Did you receive any written warnings, PIPs, or disciplinary notices? Please provide copies if available.
4. Have you made any internal complaints (HR, supervisor, ethics hotline)?
5. Were there witnesses to key events? Are any willing to speak with an attorney?
6. Have you signed an arbitration agreement or class action waiver?
7. Have you filed a charge with the EEOC or DFEH? If so, please share the charge number and date filed.
8. What outcome are you hoping to achieve?

*Intake information is protected by attorney-client privilege upon engagement.*
