# Offer Letter Generator
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-hr
**Command:** /offer-letter-generator

## What it does
Generates a professional, legally aware offer letter template from role details: title, compensation, start date, reporting structure, at-will language, and equity terms if applicable. Output is a clean draft ready for legal/HR review — not a final legal document.

## When to use
- Extending an offer to a new hire
- Standardizing offer letter format across the company
- Creating offer templates for recurring role types

## Example output
**[Company Letterhead]**

[Date]

Dear [Candidate Name],

We are pleased to offer you the position of **Senior Product Manager** at [Company Name], reporting to [Manager Name, Title]. We believe your background and experience make you an excellent fit for our team, and we're excited about what you'll bring to this role.

**Compensation and Benefits:**
- Base salary: $165,000 per year, paid bi-weekly
- Equity: 8,000 stock options, vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff, subject to Board approval and the terms of the company's equity plan
- Benefits: Health, dental, and vision insurance effective on your start date; 401(k) with 4% company match; 20 days PTO annually

**Start date:** [Date]

**Employment terms:** This offer is contingent on successful completion of a background check and your signing of our standard Confidential Information and Invention Assignment Agreement. Your employment with [Company Name] is at-will, meaning either party may end the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice.

Please sign and return this letter by [Response Deadline].

*This offer letter template is for drafting purposes only. Have your legal counsel review all offer letters before sending.*
