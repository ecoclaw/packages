# Interview Kit
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-hr
**Command:** /interview-kit

## What it does
Builds a complete interview kit for a role: a structured question bank organized by competency, a scoring rubric, interviewer guidance notes, and a debrief template. Designed to make hiring more consistent, defensible, and effective — especially for teams scaling quickly or hiring for roles they haven't filled before.

## When to use
- Preparing interviewers for a new role
- Standardizing interview processes across hiring managers
- Training new interviewers on structured interviewing

## Example output
**Interview Kit — Senior Product Manager**

**Competency: Strategic Thinking**
- Tell me about a time you had to make a significant product decision with incomplete data. Walk me through your process.
- How do you decide what NOT to build?
*What to listen for: comfort with ambiguity, structured frameworks, evidence of prioritization discipline*

**Competency: Cross-functional Influence**
- Describe a situation where you had to align engineering and marketing on a timeline they both disagreed with. What did you do?
*What to listen for: specific tactics, not just "I communicated well"*

**Competency: Customer Obsession**
- Walk me through the last time customer feedback changed your roadmap. What was the feedback, and how did you act on it?

**Scoring Rubric:**
- 4 — Exceptional: Specific example, clear impact, transferable insight
- 3 — Strong: Solid example, demonstrated competency
- 2 — Developing: Vague or generic response
- 1 — Insufficient: No relevant example or missed the point

**Debrief template:** Rate each competency 1-4 before the group debrief. State your hire/no-hire recommendation with one sentence of rationale before hearing others.
