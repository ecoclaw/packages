# Job Description Writer
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-hr
**Command:** /job-description-writer

## What it does
Generates clear, inclusive, and compelling job descriptions from a role brief. Produces a title, summary, responsibilities, requirements (hard vs. preferred), compensation range (if provided), and culture/benefits section. Applies inclusive language best practices and avoids age-biased, gendered, or unnecessarily restrictive phrasing.

## When to use
- Opening a new role or backfilling a position
- Refreshing an outdated job description
- Standardizing job description format across departments

## Example output
**Senior Product Manager — Growth**

**About the role:**
We're looking for a product manager who is energized by ambiguity, fluent in data, and ready to own the full lifecycle of our growth product surface. You'll work at the intersection of engineering, design, and go-to-market — setting direction, removing blockers, and shipping things people actually use.

**What you'll do:**
- Define and own the roadmap for user acquisition and activation features
- Partner with data and engineering to instrument, test, and iterate
- Write clear specs — and know when not to write them
- Represent the customer in every product decision

**What we're looking for:**
- 5+ years in product management, with at least 2 in a growth or PLG context
- Strong analytical skills — you can pull your own data and know what questions to ask
- Track record of shipping in fast-moving environments
- Clear written and verbal communication

**Preferred:**
- Experience with B2B SaaS products
- Familiarity with A/B testing frameworks

**Compensation:** $150,000–$175,000 base + equity + benefits
