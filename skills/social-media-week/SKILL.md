# Social Media Week

## What this skill does

Takes one topic (a blog post, a news item, a case win, a regulatory update, a firm milestone) and generates:

- **5 LinkedIn posts** — thought leadership, long-form value, professional tone
- **5 X (Twitter) posts** — punchy, shareable, hashtag-optimized
- **3 Instagram captions** — visual-first, community-oriented, slightly warmer tone

Total: 13 posts covering M–F for all three platforms.

Each post includes:
- Full caption/copy
- Suggested hashtags
- Content direction for visual (photo, graphic, or Canva template suggestion)
- Optimal posting time

## How to invoke

```
/social-media-week
```

Claude will ask:
- What's the topic or source content? (paste a URL, a blog post draft, or describe the topic)
- What outcome are we driving? (traffic, awareness, leads, engagement)
- Any specific news hook or timely angle?
- Firm tone this week: standard / more conversational / more urgent?

## Platform strategies

### LinkedIn (B2B, professional)
- 150–300 words per post
- Hook in first line (no "In today's post..." intros)
- 1 insight, 1 application, 1 CTA
- 5-8 relevant hashtags
- Post types: carousel prompt, opinion post, how-to, case outcome, regulatory alert

### X / Twitter (quick takes, news)
- Under 280 characters (leave room for replies)
- One strong point per tweet
- 2-3 hashtags max
- Thread-starter option for complex topics
- Retweet bait: statistic + opinion, or hot take

### Instagram (visual, community)
- Caption: 100-200 words
- Lead with emotion or visual hook
- 15-20 hashtags in first comment (not caption)
- Content types: infographic caption, behind-the-scenes, "Did you know?", win celebration
