# Portfolio Narrative
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-finance
**Command:** /portfolio-narrative

## What it does
Transforms portfolio performance data — returns, allocation, benchmark comparison, key contributors and detractors — into a clear, client-ready narrative for quarterly or annual reviews. Explains what happened, why it happened, and what it means for the client's plan. Avoids jargon without oversimplifying.

## When to use
- Quarterly client review preparation
- Annual performance report writing
- Explaining an underperforming period without losing client confidence

## Example output
**Q4 2025 Portfolio Review — [Client Name]**

Your portfolio returned +4.2% in Q4, compared to a +3.7% return for your blended benchmark. For the full year, your portfolio gained 11.8% versus 10.4% for the benchmark.

**What drove performance:**
The primary contributors were your equity holdings in healthcare (+9.1%) and technology (+6.8%), both of which benefited from rate stabilization and strong earnings. Your international allocation was the primary detractor (-1.2%), reflecting dollar strength and slower growth in European markets.

**What stayed on course:**
Your bond allocation provided ballast during October's volatility — exactly as designed. Your target allocation remains intact with only minor rebalancing needed.

**Looking ahead:**
No changes to your long-term strategy are warranted. We'll discuss your upcoming liquidity needs at our January meeting and adjust your cash position accordingly.

*Past performance does not guarantee future results. This summary is for informational purposes only.*
