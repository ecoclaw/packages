# Market Commentary
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-finance
**Command:** /market-commentary

## What it does
Generates timely, advisor-branded market commentary for client emails, newsletters, and social media. Takes macro inputs — key index returns, Fed activity, economic indicators, notable events — and produces clear, balanced analysis that informs without alarming.

## When to use
- Weekly or monthly client email newsletter
- Response to a client asking "should I be worried about the markets?"
- LinkedIn or social media market update posts

## Example output
**March 2026 Market Commentary**

Markets opened the year with resilience. The S&P 500 is up 3.1% year-to-date through February, with gains broadening beyond the large-cap technology names that dominated 2024-2025.

**What's driving markets:** Inflation has continued its gradual descent toward the Fed's 2% target, and while rate cuts remain data-dependent, the direction of travel is clear. Corporate earnings surprised to the upside in Q4, with 72% of S&P 500 companies beating estimates.

**Where we're watching closely:** Commercial real estate credit stress remains elevated, and geopolitical uncertainty continues to create periodic volatility. Neither appears systemic at this stage, but both warrant monitoring.

**Our takeaway for clients:** Diversified portfolios are behaving as designed. Volatility is normal — react to your plan, not to headlines.

*This commentary is for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice.*
