# Client Follow-Up
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-realestate
**Command:** /client-followup

## What it does
Drafts personalized follow-up emails and text messages for key touchpoints in the client journey: post-showing, post-offer, under-contract check-ins, and post-close thank-yous. Tone matches the relationship stage — warm and brief for texts, more detailed for emails.

## When to use
- After a showing when you want to gauge interest without being pushy
- Following a rejected offer to keep the buyer engaged
- At close to request a review or referral

## Example output
**Subject:** Great seeing you at 412 Maple — quick follow-up

Hi Sarah,

Really enjoyed walking 412 Maple with you today. I could tell the kitchen caught your attention — that renovation is genuinely one of the best I've seen at this price point.

A few things worth knowing: the sellers are motivated, there's no HOA, and the roof was replaced in 2022.

Happy to pull comps or talk numbers whenever you're ready. No pressure — just want to make sure you have what you need to decide.

Talk soon,
[Agent Name]
