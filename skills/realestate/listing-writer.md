# Listing Writer
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-realestate
**Command:** /listing-writer

## What it does
Generates compelling MLS-ready property listings from a set of inputs: address, key features, square footage, bedrooms/bathrooms, and any notable upgrades. Produces a headline, a 150-200 word description, and a bullet-point highlights section optimized for both search and buyer engagement.

## When to use
- You have a new listing and need copy fast
- Your draft description feels flat or generic
- You want multiple variations to A/B test

## Example output
**Headline:** Renovated Craftsman in Sought-After Elmwood — Private Backyard & Modern Kitchen

**Description:** Step into one of Elmwood's most thoughtfully updated homes. This 3-bed, 2-bath Craftsman blends original character — arched doorways, hardwood floors, built-in bookshelves — with a fully renovated kitchen featuring quartz countertops, custom cabinetry, and stainless appliances. The primary suite offers a spa-style bath and generous closet space. Out back, a landscaped yard with patio and mature trees creates a private retreat rare at this price point. Walk to cafés, parks, and top-rated schools. Offers reviewed Tuesday.
