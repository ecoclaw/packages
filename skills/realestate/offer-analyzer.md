# Offer Analyzer
**Package:** @gonzih/skills-realestate
**Command:** /offer-analyzer

## What it does
Takes multiple offer terms as inputs — price, down payment, financing type, contingencies, close date, escalation clauses — and produces a side-by-side comparison with a plain-language analysis of each offer's strengths, risks, and net position for the seller. Helps you present complex decisions clearly to clients.

## When to use
- Reviewing multiple offers with a seller client
- Explaining why the highest price isn't always the strongest offer
- Preparing a counteroffer strategy

## Example output
**Offer Comparison — 412 Maple Street**

| | Offer A | Offer B | Offer C |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | $950,000 | $935,000 | $960,000 |
| Down payment | 20% | 3.5% FHA | 30% |
| Contingencies | Inspection, finance | Inspection, finance, appraisal | Inspection only |
| Close | 30 days | 45 days | 21 days |

**Analysis:** Offer C's $960K price comes with the fewest contingencies and the fastest close. Despite being $10K above Offer A, the cash-heavy down payment and clean terms significantly reduce fall-through risk. Offer B's FHA financing introduces appraisal exposure in a market where this property may challenge standard comps.

**Recommendation for discussion:** Offer C represents the strongest net position for a seller prioritizing certainty.
