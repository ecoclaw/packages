# Monthly Report

## What this skill does

Produces a professional monthly marketing performance report to share with firm leadership or clients. Synthesizes data from multiple channels into a clear narrative with actionable recommendations.

Includes:
1. **Executive summary** — 3-5 sentence month-in-review
2. **Channel-by-channel performance** — SEO, social, ads, email
3. **Content produced** — what was published this month
4. **Key wins** — what worked
5. **Opportunities** — what to improve
6. **Next month's plan** — 3-5 priority actions

## How to invoke

```
/monthly-report
```

Claude will ask for metrics (paste whatever you have — Claude works with any subset):
- Website: sessions, organic traffic, new vs. returning, top pages, conversions
- SEO: keyword rankings, new rankings, featured snippets won
- Social: followers, reach, impressions, engagement rate, top posts
- Ads: impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, cost per lead
- Email: open rate, click rate, unsubscribes, replies
- Content: posts published, pages added
- Leads: total inquiries, source breakdown, consultations booked
